Wiki Loves Folklore (Meta-Wiki: Wiki Loves Folklore) is an international photographic contest organized annually at Wikimedia Commons to document folk cultures in different regions of the world. Wiki Loves Folklore 2023 is a continuation of Wiki Loves Folklore 2022 with the theme of folk culture. The origin of the project started in 2018 from Wiki Loves Love 2019 in the theme of ceremonies and festivals of love.

Scope

This photography contest is focused on folk culture of different regions on categories, such as, but not limited to, folk festivals, folk dances, folk music, folk activities, folk games, folk cuisine, folk wear, folklore, and tradition, including ballads, folktales, fairy tales, legends, traditional song and dance, folk plays, games, seasonal events, calendar customs, folk arts, folk religion, mythology etc. You can check out some other suggestions and examples in our Category page.

Prizes

• 1st prize: 500 USD

• 2nd prize: 400 USD

• 3rd prize: 300 USD

• Top 10 consolation prizes: 40 USD Each

• Best Video prize and best Audio prize: 150 USD & 150 USD

• Top uploader prize for images: First Prize: 100 USD, Second prize: 50 USD

• Wiki Loves Folklore Postcards to top 100 Uploaders

• Certificates and postcards to Local Organizers.

(Disclaimer: Prizes to be dispersed in a gift card or voucher format.)

Timeline

The timeline for WLF 2023 is as follows

  • February 1 – 31 March , 2023
  • Start for submissions: February 1, 2023 00:01 (UTC)
  • Deadline for submissions: March 31, 2023 23:59 (UTC)
  • Results Declaration: To be declared (Note: Result declaration is subject to changes and may be delayed subject to circumstances with pandemic.)

What we won't accept

We won't accept media that are:

  • Out of Commons Project scope.
  • Which lack EXIF information.
  • that are excessive processed and digitally altered.
  • Pornographic and explicit images.
  • Artwork due to copyright issues, etc.
